Project Proposal

June 2025 Completed

The Project Proposal milestone marks the formal inception of the Midwify research endeavour. The team reviewed existing literature, identified six critical research gaps in Sri Lankan midwifery, and formulated a clear research problem statement. Objectives for all four sub-projects were defined and a comprehensive proposal document and project charter were accepted by the supervising committee.

✅

Progress Presentation I

August 2025 Completed

This milestone showcases the first functional prototypes of all four components. Initial datasets collected and preprocessed; baseline ML models for maternal risk and CTG classification trained; UI wireframes finalised in Figma; and the Unity VR scene in active development. Live prototype walkthroughs presented to the supervising panel for formative feedback.

✅

Progress Presentation II

October 2025 Completed

Significantly refined systems incorporating panel feedback. All four applications feature-complete, model accuracy benchmarks reported, and initial SUS usability testing with midwifery students presented. The VR environment fully navigable, and the bilingual infant cranial app tested with Sinhala- and Tamil-speaking participants.

🏆

Final Assessment

November 2025 Upcoming

Submission of the complete, working research system and full dissertation. All four digital tools deployed, tested, and documented to production-ready standards. The dissertation presents methodology, results, usability findings, limitations, and future directions, adhering to SLIIT Faculty of Computing academic research guidelines.

🎤

Viva Voce

December 2025 Pending

The oral examination in which each team member defends their research contributions before a panel of academic staff and external examiners. Candidates are assessed on domain understanding, critical analysis of design choices, interpretation of evaluation results, and ability to respond to technical scrutiny. Concludes formal academic assessment of Midwify.
