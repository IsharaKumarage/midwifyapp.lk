Abstract

Bridging the Gap in Maternal Healthcare

Sri Lanka's midwifery care system faces a critical digital divide. Frontline midwives lack access to intelligent, real-time tools capable of detecting maternal risk factors, monitoring infant cranial development, interpreting complex fetal health signals, and receiving immersive practical training. Midwify directly confronts these systemic shortcomings by engineering four synergistic digital solutions tailored for the Sri Lankan healthcare context.

The Midwify platform comprises: a machine-learning-powered mobile app for real-time maternal risk prediction; a bilingual AI platform for infant cranial deformity and posture monitoring; an automated fetal health assessment system interpreting CTG data; and an immersive VR training environment combined with a postnatal care mobile app — delivering Sri Lanka's first offline-capable, multilingual digital midwifery ecosystem.

Our Research

Four Integrated Components

Each component targets a distinct clinical need, together forming a complete digital midwifery ecosystem.

📱
Component 01

ML Maternal Risk Prediction

Real-time maternal risk classification for midwives using ensemble ML models deployed on a Flutter mobile app with offline TFLite inference.

🤖 Random Forest 📊 XGBoost 📱 Flutter 🔥 Firebase ⚡ TFLite
👶
Component 02

AI Infant Cranial Monitoring

Bilingual (Sinhala/Tamil) AI app detecting infant cranial deformity and postural imbalance using CNN-based computer vision analysis.

🧠 CNN 👁️ Computer Vision 🌐 Bilingual 📱 React Native
🫀
Component 03

Automated Fetal CTG Assessment

Automated CTG data interpretation classifying fetal health into Normal, Suspect, or Pathological states — reducing diagnostic error.

📈 CTG Analysis 🤖 SVM 🐍 Scikit-learn 📊 Chart.js
🥽
Component 04

VR Training + Postnatal App

Immersive Unity/OpenXR VR simulation for newborn handling training plus a Flutter postnatal care mobile companion app.

🎮 Unity 🥽 OpenXR 🎨 Blender 🗣️ Speech API
